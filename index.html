Our Story

charity: water is a nonprofit organization founded in 2006 by Scott Harrison, who made a simple but life-changing decision: to dedicate his 31st birthday to raising funds for clean water. One act of generosity grew into a global movement to end the water crisis. We partner with local organizations and supporters to bring clean, sustainable water sources to developing countries. Over the years, charity: water has funded thousands of water projects across dozens of countries, serving millions of people. Together, through simple and generous acts, we can make a lasting difference in helping everyone, everywhere, have access to clean water.

The Real Impact You Make Around the World

Families across the world walk miles to collect water that isn’t safe to drink. Your donation helps end that struggle by providing clean, reliable water to families and communities in need. With clean water, people can live healthier, more fulfilling lives—gaining time, opportunities, and hope for the future.
